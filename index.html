Philippine Tourist Destinations

Baguio

Baguio is home of the Tropical Pine Forest housing Pines hence the other name "The city of Pines". Baguio is also the home of beautiful plants that in normal circumstances woudn't survive anywhere else in the Philippines because of its cold, and windy climate. Tourists spots in Baguio such as the strawberry farm, the mansion, and nowadays the night market are what attracts tourists.


Boracay

Boracay is known to be one of the most popular tourist spots in the Philippines. It is famous for its white, sandy beaches, clear water, and its sunset.


Cebu

Cebu is known for its picture perfect and instagrammable beaches. It is also known to be the largest island in the Visayas region. It is rich in Spanish colonial history with its urban buildings.It is also known to have best diving activities such as scuba diving,etc.
